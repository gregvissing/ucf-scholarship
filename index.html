Empower Our Next Leaders

From the classrooms and labs to co-ops and on-the-job learning, UC students are shaping the future.

These are the learners who will become leaders. These are the generational trailblazers, big-picture problem-solvers and global innovators our world needs now more than ever—and you can help them get there.

Through Next, Now: The Campaign for Cincinnati, you can empower our next leaders with your support for scholarships and fellowships. With your help, together we can give students the opportunities to write their stories of success here at UC, and well beyond.

WHY NOW?

In today’s knowledge economy, the value of a degree from UC—a Carnegie R1 institution—has never been higher. -or- In today’s knowledge economy, less than 30% of Ohioans have a bachelor’s degree or higher.

Even with public funding reductions, UC continues to contain student costs with its Tuition Guarantee Program. -or- Ohio is ranked 45th out of 50 states for college affordability. -or- Nearly [two-thirds?] of UC students have unmet financial need.

Lacking financial resources remain a hurdle to students attending college, particularly for underserved populations. -or- UC has permanently increased scholarship support for underrepresented minority undergraduate students by $500,000 per year.

Scholarship & Fellowship Priority Areas

ACCESSIBILITY

Nearly 30% of UC students are first in their family to go to college. Your support for accessibility scholarships opens more doors for those who aspire to learn, do and dream.

AFFORDABILITY

The average unmet financial need for UC students is $XX. Your support for affordability-focused scholarships can ensure that students can attend, grow and graduate as Bearcats.

EXCELLENCE

UC is home to 14 colleges and academic programs of national/global distinction. Your support for excellence-centered scholarships attracts brilliant students of all backgrounds to bring their talents to UC.
